A little bit about me.

I've made my home base in sunny Los Angeles, California. My tech journey began as a young blogger figuring out how to personalize my own small website. Curiosity pushed me to find ways to change colors, add backgrounds, and bring my words to life with fun visuals. The world of code intrigued me, and I had to learn more.

As I took classes, I realized that coding not only satisfied my curiosity, but I had found something that gave me the freedom and versatility I was looking for in my career. Now I've made a full switch to front-end development, where I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.
